Pre-installation: Providing perfect guidance to our clients, in reference to, making choices out of the world’s best surveillance equipment’s and products available in the market, which we provide at a good competitive price in the competitive market.

Installation: We set the surveillance design of the cliental site, in such manner, that covers maximum area with minimum surveillance equipment and products to be installed, which proves to be very cost effective to the clients.
